Film at Lincoln Center’s Elinor Bunin Munroe Film Center will reopen on April 16. The Walter Reade Theater will reopen in the weeks following, after minor renovations are completed.

The FLC Virtual Cinema will remain open, allowing movie lovers across the country to continue to enjoy the organization’s unparalleled programming from the comfort of their homes.

The well-being of our audience and staff remains our top priority, which is why we have developed a comprehensive series of health and safety policies in coordination with state, city, and medical experts, including staff training, reduced capacity, strict social distancing measures in auditoriums and all public spacing, enhanced cleaning and disinfecting measures, hand-sanitizing stations, reserved seating, contactless arrival, and mandatory masks at all times. Policies will be strictly enforced for the protection of our staff and guests. Management reserves the right to deny admission or dismiss any customer for noncompliance with FLC policies. A digital gift certificate of equal value was sent to the email address associated with your order in spring 2020 and may be redeemed online for any upcoming screenings, subject to availability, or be applied toward a new or renewing FLC membership. If you did not receive your gift certificate or have misplaced the email, please contact ticketing@filmlinc.org. How to use your digital gift certificate: Log in to your account. Add the desired screenings to your cart and select Check Out. Once you are in the Payment window, scroll down to Apply Gift Certificate and enter your Gift Certificate number exactly as it appears. Then select Apply Gift Certificate, which will subtract the gift certificate value from the current cart total. Finally, select Submit. If there is a balance left, you will be prompted to enter additional payment information. Once you apply that payment, your order is complete.
